"Beyond One Health"

2026–present

Planning a research programme on institutional design for governing humans, animals, and digital minds together. Themes include representing nonhuman interests, mapping co-benefit opportunities, and analysing edge cases in joint governance. Mentoring two fellows at the Sentient Futures Project Incubator on AI sentience and animal welfare.
